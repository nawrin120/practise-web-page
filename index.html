A fish in an animal which lives in water. There are more than 30,000 species of fish living in the water bodies. Fish differs in the shape, size and colour. They all live in different part of the oceans, depending on their habitat. Some fishes even stays at the bottom of the oceans throughout their life. Fish breathe through their gills. They consume water from their mouth and pass it through their gills passage to breathe. “Gills are feathery organs full of blood vessel”. A fish use its fins to breathe.
